Coquito

Description

Coquito is a traditional Puerto Rican coconut-based alcoholic beverage, typically served during the Christmas season. It is often referred to as "Puerto Rican eggnog" due to its creamy texture and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (12 oz) evaporated milk
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (15 oz) cream of coconut
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup white rum
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a serving container.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
