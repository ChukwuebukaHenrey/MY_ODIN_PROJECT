Yam Casserole

Description

Yam casserole is a traditional dish made with yams, often served during holidays.

Ingredients

  • Yams
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Cook the yams until tender.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan and add sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Combine the cooked yams with the butter mixture.
  4. Pour the mixture into a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
