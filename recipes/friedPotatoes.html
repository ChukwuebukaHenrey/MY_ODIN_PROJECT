Fried Potatoes

Description

Fried potatoes are a simple and delicious side dish made by frying sliced potatoes in oil until crispy.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Scent Leaves

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into fry-shaped pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer to 375Â°F.
  3. Add the potato pieces to the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Remove the fried potatoes from the oil and drain on paper towels.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped scent leaves if desired.
