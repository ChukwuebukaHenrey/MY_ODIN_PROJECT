Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta that is made with layers of flat noodles and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer the noodles, beef sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes or until golden brown.
