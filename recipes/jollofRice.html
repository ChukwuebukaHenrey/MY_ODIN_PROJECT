Description

Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. It is often served at parties and celebrations and is known for its vibrant red color and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3 chicken thighs, cut into small chunks
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and bell peppers, cook until softened.
  3. Add tomatoes and tomato paste, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add chicken broth, thyme, curry powder, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add chicken pieces and cook until no longer pink.
  7. Add rice to the pot and stir to combine.
  8. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked through.
